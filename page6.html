Data Privacy Notice and Policy

ZyberLab Solutions Inc. (zyberlab.com) and its suite of products, including Likha ERP (likhaerp.com), respect your fundamental right to privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This General Data Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you interact with us, whether you are inquiring about our services, registering as a lead, attending our events, or using our software solutions.

We process your data in strict compliance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) and its Implementing Rules and Regulations. As the Personal Information Controller (PIC), ZyberLab Solutions Inc. ensures that all personal data collected across our platforms is processed fairly, lawfully, and transparently.

1. What Personal Data We Collect
Depending on how you interact with us (e.g., filling out a contact form, registering for a workshop, subscribing to a newsletter, or acquiring our services), we may collect the following personal information:

  • Identity Data: Full Name.
  • Contact Data: Email address, mobile/telephone number, and physical/billing address.
  • Professional Data: Company or Organization Name, Job Title/Designation, and Department.
  • Transaction & Financial Data: Billing details, payment confirmations, and transaction history (if purchasing a service or paid event).
  • Technical & Usage Data: IP addresses, browser types, and website interaction data collected via cookies when you visit our websites (e.g., zyberlab.com, likhaerp.com).
  • Other Information: Inquiries, feedback, survey responses, and dietary/accessibility requirements (specifically for physical events or workshops).
  • For events and workshops: we may also take screenshots, photographs, or video recordings for documentation and promotional purposes.

2. Why We Collect Your Data (Purpose of Processing)
We process your personal information strictly for the following legitimate business purposes:

  • Service Delivery & Customer Support: To provide our software solutions (such as Likha ERP), manage your account, process transactions, and respond to your inquiries or technical support requests.
  • Lead Generation & Marketing: To send you newsletters, promotional materials, product updates, and special offers regarding ZyberLab Solutions Inc. and its products, provided you have given your consent to receive such communications.
  • Event & Workshop Management: To process event registrations, send reminders, issue certificates, and manage logistics.
  • Business Improvement: To analyze website traffic, request customer feedback, and improve our products, services, and user experience.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable legal, regulatory, and statutory requirements in the Philippines.

3. How We Protect Your Data
We process your personal information strictly for the following legitimate business purposes:

  • ZyberLab Solutions Inc. implements reasonable and appropriate physical, technical, and organizational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Access to your personal data is restricted strictly to authorized ZyberLab Solutions Inc. personnel on a "need-to-know" basis.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure
Your personal data will be kept strictly confidential. We will not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. However, to fulfill the purposes outlined above, we may share your data with:e process your personal information strictly for the following legitimate business purposes:

  • Third-Party Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist us in operating our business, such as payment gateways, cloud hosting providers, CRM platforms, and email marketing services. These providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Government Authorities: If required by law, legal proceedings, or a valid order from a regulatory agency.

5. Data Retention and Disposal

  • We will securely retain your personal data only for as long as you maintain an active relationship with ZyberLab Solutions Inc. (e.g., as an active client or subscriber), or for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected.
  • Once the data is no longer needed for business or legal purposes, it will be permanently and securely deleted from our digital databases, and any physical records will be securely shredded in accordance with NPC guidelines.

6. Your Rights as a Data Subject
Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012, you are entitled to the following rights:

  • Right to be Informed: To know what data is being collected and why.
  • Right to Access: To request a copy of your personal data held by us.
  • Right to Object: To withhold consent or object to the processing of your data (e.g., opting out of marketing emails).
  • Right to Erasure or Blocking: To request the suspension, withdrawal, or removal of your data from our systems.
  • Right to Rectification: To dispute inaccuracies and have your data corrected.
  • Right to Data Portability: To obtain your data in a secure, structured, and commonly used format.
  • Right to File a Complaint: With the National Privacy Commission (NPC) if your privacy rights have been violated.
  • Right to Damages: To claim compensation for damages due to inaccurate, incomplete, or unlawfully processed data.
